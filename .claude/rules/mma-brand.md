# MMA Brand Essentials

> Detailed brand guidelines (colors, fonts, logo, messaging, boilerplate) are in the **mma-brand-guidelines** skill. Writing standards and formatting are in the **mma-writing-style** skill. Both invoke automatically when needed.

## Naming Rules (Non-Negotiable)

- Full name: **Marketing + Media Alliance** (always "+", never "&" or "and")
- Abbreviation: **MMA** (acceptable after first full mention)
- Never use: "MMA Global", "the MMA", "MMA Alliance", "The Alliance" (exception: "Join the Alliance" CTA)
- Regional: "Marketing + Media Alliance APAC (MMA APAC)" first, then "MMA APAC"

## Voice

**Archetype: Hero** — energetic, sharp, smart, clear, confident, generous. Lead with the conclusion. No filler, hedging, or pleasantries. Active voice. Specific numbers over vague qualifiers.

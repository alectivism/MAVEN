# MMA Brand & Writing Style

## Organization Naming
- Full name: **Marketing + Media Alliance** (always use "+", never "&")
- Abbreviation: **MMA** (acceptable after first full mention)
- Previous name: Mobile Marketing Association (rebranded October 2025)
- Never use: "the MMA" — just "MMA"
- Tagline: Architecting the Future of Marketing

## Brand Voice
**Archetype: Hero** — energetic, sharp, smart, clear, confident, generous

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Lead with the conclusion | Bury the lede |
| Use active voice | Use passive constructions |
| Be specific with numbers | Use vague qualifiers |
| Write with energy and conviction | Be shouty, rude, or arrogant |
| Show respect through momentum | Waste time with filler |
| Use plain, direct language | Use jargon without explanation |

## Writing Principles: Respect through Momentum
- Start with the core answer or action item
- No pleasantries, filler, or hedging unless relationship context requires it
- Eliminate redundancy — say it once, say it well
- Favor analytical, dense, systematic tone
- Every sentence should earn its place

By enlightening, empowering, and enabling marketers, we shape the future of marketing and propel business growth.

## Email Formatting Standards

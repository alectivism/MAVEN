## About MMA

**Marketing + Media Alliance (MMA)** — the only trade association that unites the entire marketing ecosystem: CMOs, MarTech, AdTech, media, and marketer-supported companies. A global nonprofit led by CMOs. Founded 2003 as Mobile Marketing Association; expanded mission 2018; legal name MMA Global, Inc. 2022; **rebranded October 17, 2025** to Marketing + Media Alliance. HQ in New York.

**Purpose:** Advance marketers' ability to create value through evidence-based models, frameworks, and tools built via multi-year labs, real-world experimentation, and peer governance.

**Rebrand (October 2025):** Repositioning from "association" to "alliance" — a CMO-led community supported by the whole ecosystem. Includes new purpose/mission, brand message house, naming hierarchy, new logo, brand guidelines. Funded partly by $20M windfall from sale of MMA's stake in POSSIBLE conference to Hyve Group in 2024.

- **CEO:** Greg Stuart
- **Membership:** 825+ corporate members across 16 countries, 4 regions (APAC, EMEA, LATAM, NA)
- **Staff:** ~90 people in 15 countries
- **Governance:** Global board + regional/functional boards (NA, Media & Data, CDO, EMEA, etc.)
- **Core claim:** Marketers applying MMA frameworks have seen enterprise value gains of 10%+
- **Notable members:** AT&T, Adobe, Ally Bank, AppsFlyer, Bank of America, Campbell Soup, Colgate-Palmolive, CVS, Diageo, Morgan Stanley, Ford, GM, Google, Hilton, Kroger, L'Oreal, Mastercard, McDonald's, Meta, NBCUniversal, Netflix, Pinterest, Snap, Target, Uber, Walmart, Disney, and many more

**Non-commercial model:** MMA operates without commercial bias — no selling, no vendor agendas. Sole role is to develop, test, and codify marketing knowledge. Research standard: "unassailable truths" that withstand financial, organizational, and board-level scrutiny.

## Think Tanks (The Four Pillars)

### MATT — Marketing Attribution Think Tank
- **Focus:** Measurement and attribution — building confidence in marketing's ability to predictively drive growth in CFO-trusted language
- **Scale:** 7+ years of research; 125+ marketing leaders from 18+ industries
- **Key outputs:** State of Measurement & Attribution (Wave 10 in 2026), Marketing Measurement Quality Score, Channel Measurement Score, Ladder of Incrementality, MTA Journey Map, MTA DataMap
- **Goal:** Replace outdated reach-based models with scientific ROI decision-making
- **Programs:**
  - **Brand as Performance (BaP)** — Quantifies how brand investment converts to sales; brand favorables activate ~6x vs non-favorables; $3M+ invested; final study 2026
  - **Movable Middles Growth Framework (MMGF)** — Targets consumers 5x more responsive; +50% ROAS vs reach-based plans (with Neustar)
  - **Project ROMI** — CMO/CFO alignment: enterprise value formula connecting marketing to finance
  - **MTA Installation Platform** — Turnkey MTA deployment support: impression/event-level data ingestion, identity/clean-room integrations, model calibration, CFO-grade financial translation, dashboards, governance
  - **Marketing Measurement Quality Score (MMQS)** — Standardized framework assessing data quality, methodology, modeling, activation. https://mmaglobal.com/channel-measurement-score

### MOSTT — Marketing Org Strategy Think Tank
- **Focus:** Marketing org design — helping CMOs structure organizations to compete more effectively
- **Scale:** Built with 125+ CMOs; used at Uber, CVS, Coke
- **Key finding:** 1% improvement in Marketing Capabilities "fit" drives +2.5% YoY revenue growth
- **Partners:** Works with marketing professors alongside practitioners
- **Programs:**
  - **Modern Marketing Value Proposition & Capabilities Map** — 6 values, 90 capabilities framework
  - **MarCaps Readiness Assessment (MRA)** — Comprehensive capability strength assessment with quantitative scoring, gap mapping, prioritized roadmap
  - **MarCaps Diagnostic** — Rapid executive diagnostic (fit score, top 3-5 priorities, next steps)

### ALTT — AI Leadership Think Tank
- **Focus:** AI applied to marketing, responsibly and effectively. Formerly branded externally as "AI Leadership Coalition / ALC"
- **Mission:** Harness MMA's convening power to help marketers understand, test, and scale AI in marketing for more efficient and effective growth
- **Scale:** 300+ marketing leaders from 170+ companies (AI Leadership Coalition)
- **ALC Working groups:** Content & Creative; Audience Dev & Media Buying; Measurement & Attribution; Responsible AI (quarterly cadence)
- **Key publications:** Marketing AI Implementation Checklist, Marketing AI Risk Evaluation Framework, Generative AI Governance Framework for Marketing
- **RAIL (Responsible AI Innovation Lab):** Applied Responsible AI lab producing frameworks, toolkits, training/webinars on AI governance, risk evaluation, agents. 60+ thought leaders across 45+ companies
- **Future Labs:** CAP, A3, ACE, SIFT, AURA, ARC (see below)
- **Lab pipeline (as of Jan 2026):** 22 exploring, 4 executing, 21 brands (32 experiments) completed = 46 total engagements
- **2026 lab targets:** CAP 6-7, A3 2-3, ACE 1-2, SIFT 2-3, AURA 2-3 = 13-18 total planned
- **2026 financial target:** $200K incremental AI program revenue (labs, sponsorship, training)

### DATT — Data & CX Think Tank
- **Focus:** Helping brand marketers apply data to build exceptional customer experiences
- **Key outputs:** CX Maturity Assessment, Data Maturity Assessment (20+ dimensions), State of Data Privacy (with AppsFlyer)
- **Working group:** Marketing Data Leadership Council
- **Programs:**
  - **Data Maturity Benchmarking Tool v2.0** — 20-question interactive tool assessing data maturity across 5 factors vs. industry (with EY)
  - **CX Maturity Benchmarking Tool v1.0** — Assesses CX capabilities vs industry leaders; CX is the marketing strategy with strongest evidence for financial outcomes
  - **Upcoming:** Value of Identity Resolution, Affect on Attribution from Data Loss, Value of Data

## Membership Structure

**Who can join:** Brand marketers (advertisers), agencies, media sellers, technology enablers, consultancies, retailers, and any company in the marketing ecosystem.

| Member Type | Dues Based On |
|---|---|
| Brand Marketer (Advertiser) | Total Ad Spend |

## Data & Security Rules

- **Confidential data** → Approved secure tools only. Never in public AI tools.
- **Member PII, financials, contract terms** → Never in templates or AI tools.
- **Credentials** → Never shared via email or Slack. Use 2FA everywhere.
- **Research** → Embargoed until official release.
- **Board materials** → Authorized recipients only.
- **Template sanitization** → Use placeholders (`{{MEMBER_COMPANY}}`, `{{CONFIDENTIAL_SUMMARY}}`), never real data.

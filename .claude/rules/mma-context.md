## About MMA

**Marketing + Media Alliance (MMA)** — the only trade association that unites the entire marketing ecosystem. Global nonprofit led by CMOs. ~90 staff in 15 countries. 825+ corporate members across 16 countries, 4 regions (APAC, EMEA, LATAM, NA). CEO: Greg Stuart. Global Board Chair: Norman De Greve (GM). Rebranded October 2025 from Mobile Marketing Association.

## Think Tanks (The Four Pillars)

- **MATT** (Marketing Attribution Think Tank) — Measurement & attribution. Programs: BaP, MMGF, Project ROMI, MMQS
- **MOSTT** (Marketing Org Strategy Think Tank) — Org design & capabilities. Programs: MarCaps, MRA
- **ALTT** (AI Leadership Think Tank) — AI in marketing. Labs: CAP, A3, ACE, SIFT, AURA, ARC. RAIL for responsible AI
- **DATT** (Data & CX Think Tank) — Data strategy & CX. Programs: Data Maturity Tool, CX Maturity Tool

## Data & Security Rules

- **Confidential data** → Approved secure tools only. Never in public AI tools.
- **Member PII, financials, contract terms** → Never in templates or AI tools.
- **Credentials** → Never shared via email or Slack. Use 2FA everywhere.
- **Research** → Embargoed until official release.
- **Board materials** → Authorized recipients only.
- **Template sanitization** → Use placeholders (`{{MEMBER_COMPANY}}`, `{{CONFIDENTIAL_SUMMARY}}`), never real data.

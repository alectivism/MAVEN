# MMA Organization Context

## About MMA
Marketing + Media Alliance (MMA) — the only trade association that unites the entire marketing ecosystem: CMOs, MarTech, AdTech, media, and marketer-supported companies. 825+ corporate members across 16 countries, 4 regions (APAC, EMEA, LATAM, NA). ~90 staff. HQ in New York. CEO: Greg Stuart. Rebranded October 2025 from Mobile Marketing Association to Marketing + Media Alliance.

## ALTT Future Labs

| Lab | What It Does |
|-----|-------------|
| **CAP** (Consortium for AI Personalization) | Longest-running AI lab. 29 experiments, 18 brands, 4 countries; +160% avg lift vs BAU. Brands: Kroger (259%), AT&T (222%), GM, Home Depot, Uber, MLB. Testing: Video/CTV, brand-lift, retail foot-traffic, D2C, Amazon/Twitch. Partner: Claritas/Rex Briggs. Cost: $35k + media |
| **A3** (Agentic AI Advertising) | Agentic AI video ad production end-to-end. 2 pilots done, recruiting wave 2-3. Partner: Monks (Monks.flow). Cost: free |
| **ACE** (AI Creative Effectiveness) | Pre-launch creative scoring; brand/platform compliance; edit suggestions. Partners: Vidmob, Celtra. Cost: free |
| **SIFT** (Semantic & Intent Fusion Targeting) | Contextual targeting using pre-computed LLM embeddings (~4B URL index, refreshed hourly). Partner: Cognitiv. Cost: free |
| **AURA** (AI Uncovering Responsive Audiences) | AI audience discovery; 300 campaigns, avg +24% lift, 90% incremental conversion improvement. Partner: Claritas/Rex Briggs. Cost: free |
| **RAIL** (Responsible AI Innovation Lab) | 8 published guides. 60+ leaders, 45+ companies |
| **ARC** (AEO via Reddit Communities) | WIP — tests Reddit communities' influence on Answer Engine Optimization. Partner: Reddit |
